History of AI:

The term 'AI' was coined in 1956 at a Dartmouth conference by John McCarthy, a visionary mathematician and computer scientist. Since then AI has skyrocketed and become the most used innovation in our entire world.

Advantages of AI:

  1. Efficiency and Automation: AI streamlines tasks by automating repetitive processes. It can handle large volumes of data quickly, reducing human error and saving time.
  2. 24/7 Availability: Unlike humans, AI operates continuously without breaks or downtime. It's always ready to assist, analyze data, or perform tasks.

Disadvantages of AI:

  1. Job Displacement: Automation may lead to job losses in certain industries.
  2. Lack of Human-Like Creativity and Empathy: While AI can perform specific tasks, it lacks the emotional intelligence and creativity inherent in humans.
